Akhan Naming

Would you love to know what your Ghananian name would be?Then this is the place for you. Akan names come from a traditional naming system for people from countries such as Togo, Ivory Coast and Ghana.The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born as shown;

Days Boy's Name Girl's Name
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ama
